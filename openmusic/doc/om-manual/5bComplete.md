  1. Midicent

A midicent is a unit representing one cent of the usual MIDI pitch unit, that
is, a half-tone. For instance, a C4 corresponds to 60 in MIDI, and to 6000
midicents, C4# to 61, i.e. 6100 midicents, and so on.
